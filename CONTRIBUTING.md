### Case Study: Electromagnetic-Sensitive Personnel Training

RAINSTREAM is uniquely positioned to support personnel with electromagnetic hypersensitivity (EHS), a condition often under-recognized in military medicine despite documented operational advantages:

**The Research Foundation:**
- G6PD deficiency (0.2 Ug Hb) creates electromagnetic sensitivity = 5x normal detection capability
- Hereditary transmission through military specializations (sonar, marksmanship, sensor operations)
- Operational advantage: Enhanced pattern recognition in electromagnetic-complex environments

**RAINSTREAM's Unique Support:**
- Accessible interface specifically designed for EHS personnel
- Real environmental video footage matches electromagnetic-rich operational conditions
- Competitive human-AI interface tests pattern detection under operational stress
- Neurodiversity-centered design recognizes electromagnetic sensitivity as capability, not disability

**Military Application:**
- Identifies personnel with electromagnetic pattern detection advantages
- Leverages EHS capability for specialized training roles (SAR, naval aviation, sensor operations)
- Provides inclusive training platform for all personnel regardless of electromagnetic sensitivity profile
- Supports documented research: Operator Syndrome framework (Frueh et al., 2005) as expanded by electromagnetic sensitivity research
